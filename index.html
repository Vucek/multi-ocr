1
A new tab opened with ndk.cz login.
Complete the eduID login there.
Select your institution, enter credentials.
2
After login, open DevTools on the ndk.cz tab:
Cmd+Option+I (Mac) or F12 (Win)
3
Go to Application tab → Cookies → https://ndk.cz
The session cookies are HttpOnly (invisible to document.cookie).
You must copy them from the Application tab.
4
Find JSESSIONID and copy its Value.
Also copy any _shibsession_* cookie values.
Paste below as: JSESSIONID=VALUE; _shibsession_xxx=VALUE
Or use the Network tab: pick any request to ndk.cz, copy the full Cookie header from Request Headers.
